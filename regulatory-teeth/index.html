Regulatory Teeth

Prepared by Quinn Campbell · March 2026 · For Ben Chen & Jeff Wu
Current State

Jeff, this section is for you. Current state: Vietnam is solidly Phase 1 in your framework. No platform has been fined, warned, or blocked for child safety or age verification. Le Ton Viet confirmed: zero enforcement actions so far and the government "still doesn't know how to actively" investigate age verification.

Teeth Are Coming — H2 2026

But Le Ton Viet believes the teeth are coming in H2 2026. The PDPL sanctions decree completed public consultation on March 26 and is expected to be finalized soon. Once enacted, revenue-based penalties up to 5% of annual revenue become enforceable.

Viet's direct assessment: "I think the enforcement would actually get more intense after the election in the next few months." He expects enforcement to intensify in H2 2026 once post-election stability takes hold.

The market will unlock starting then. We need to be laying the groundwork now to be ready for it.

Live Signal: VNG / Roblox

The VNG/Roblox situation is a live example of this dynamic — the PDPL is already creating real compliance problems for major platforms, even before penalties are finalized. This is the early signal.
